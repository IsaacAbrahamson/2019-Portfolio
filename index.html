About me

Designing and implementing creative software isn't something new to me. At just five and six, I would spend time drawing out website and game ideas on paper. When I was about ten, I built a "functional" website by linking together HTML pages in MS Publisher. At fourteen, I built and ran a Minecraft server with custom modifications, and when I was fifteen, I took the motivation and resourcefulness I had learned as a homeschooler and taught myself how to program and build websites. Right now I'm a systems analyst intern for SPARK Business Works and a college student studying Computer Science and Business.

DOM library

I had an idea to build myself a custom dashboard, but I needed a way to navigate several pages on it. I looked at some existing libraries, but they were all too complicated, so I started my own. Sideways creates fullscreen pages based on HTML markup and gives you a JavaScript library to easily change and interact with them. This project is still in the early stages, but it has most of the critical features complete.
